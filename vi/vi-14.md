# Description

# Clean Script
You get a message from the school.

New rule.
Changed pickup time.
Different uniform day.
One paragraph written like everyone already knows what is going on.

And if you read it wrong, that is on you.

That is how ordinary people get worn down.
Not by big disasters.
By constant little frictions.

So before you guess, you paste the message into AI.
You ask it to rewrite it clearly.
Tell you the key action.
Tell you the date.
Tell you what actually matters.

Suddenly it is simple.
No fog.
No missed pickup.
No embarrassed phone call.

That is what people miss about AI.
Sometimes the win is not dramatic.
Sometimes it is just your day not falling apart.

Just ask AI.
