# Clean Script
Insurance only feels simple when you are paying for it.

The moment you need to understand it, the language gets slippery.
Conditions.
Exclusions.
Waiting periods.
Phrases that sound harmless until they cost you.

And that is not an accident.

So before anything goes wrong, you ask AI to walk through the policy with you.
What is covered.
What is not.
What conditions might trip you up.
What questions you should ask the insurer now, not later.

That is the difference between owning a policy and understanding one.
And those are not the same thing.

Most people only discover that when it is too late.
You do not have to be most people.

Just ask AI.
