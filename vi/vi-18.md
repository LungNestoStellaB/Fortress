# Description
Scams often win because they create urgency before people can think. This script gives viewers a simple pause button.

# Clean Script
You get a text.

Package delayed.
Bank account issue.
Road toll unpaid.
Click here now.

Everything about it is designed to do one thing.
Make you react before you think.

And that is the whole game.
Not intelligence.
Speed.

So you slow the moment down.
You paste the message into AI.
You ask what the red flags are.
What the scam pattern is.
What you should do next.

Now the spell is broken.
Now it looks obvious.
The fake urgency.
The weird link.
The language that does not quite sit right.

That is why I keep saying this.
AI is not just about doing things faster.
Sometimes it stops you doing something stupid under pressure.

Just ask AI.
