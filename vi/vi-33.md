# Title
The Online Checkout That Felt Risky

# Description
Suspicious checkout pages make people abandon purchases or take bad risks. This script shows how AI can help them assess trust signals before they pay.

# Clean Script
You are halfway through buying something online.

Then the page changes.
Looks a bit off.
Branding is strange.
Payment screen feels cheap.
And now you have that tight feeling in your chest.

Should I trust this or not?

That question matters.
Because the internet is full of places that look just real enough.

So you stop and ask AI.
You show it the page.
You ask what trust signals are present.
What red flags to look for.
Whether the wording, layout, or process suggests trouble.

That pause can save a lot of pain.
A bad purchase is annoying.
A stolen card is a week of your life.

You do not need to be an expert.
You need a second set of eyes.

Just ask AI.
