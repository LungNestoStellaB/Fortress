# Clean Script
There is always one letter you keep putting off.

Complaint.
Boundary.
Request.
Something you need to say properly because getting it wrong will make a mess.

So you leave it.
Not because you are lazy.
Because tone is tricky.
Too soft and nothing happens.
Too hard and now you have a fight.

This is where AI becomes a lifeline.
You explain the situation.
What happened.
What outcome you want.
What tone you need.

Then it gives you a draft.
Not perfect truth from the heavens.
A starting point.
A structure.
Words that get you moving.

And that is often all people need.
Not genius.
Momentum.

Just ask AI.
