# Title
The Bill You Knew Was Wrong But Could Not Prove

# Description
Billing disputes often depend on confidence more than facts. This script shows how AI can turn a vague feeling into a clear case.

# Clean Script
You open a bill and something feels off.

Not wildly off.
Just wrong enough to bother you.
A charge you do not recognise.
A fee nobody mentioned.
A total that does not match the story you were told.

And now you are expected to challenge it from memory.

That is where people give up.
Not because they are wrong.
Because the company is counting on fatigue.

So you feed the bill into AI.
You ask it to spot unusual charges.
Summarise the problem.
Draft a clean message asking for a review.

Now you are not ringing up annoyed and half-prepared.
Now you have a case.
A calm one.
A clear one.
A harder one to brush aside.

Just ask AI.
