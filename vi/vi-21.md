You look up a recipe.

It says things like fold gently, reduce slightly, finish under the grill.

So now you do something smarter.
You ask AI to translate the recipe into plain English.
Explain the cooking terms.
Tell you what the step should actually look like.
Tell you what mistake ruins it.

That changes the whole mood.
You stop feeling behind.
You start cooking with confidence.

And that matters more than people think.
Because confidence is often the only thing standing between you and learning something new.

Just ask AI.
