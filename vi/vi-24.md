# Description

# Clean Script
A lot of people think they are getting worse with technology.

Half the time, that is not true.
Half the time the device is just badly set up.
Text too small.
Buttons hard to see.
Notifications everywhere.
Settings buried six menus deep like a bad joke.

And nobody tells you what is there.

So you ask AI a better question.
How do I make my phone easier to read, easier to hear, and less annoying to use?
Then you tell it which phone you have.

Now you start finding the good stuff.
Larger text.
Voice typing.
Magnifier.
Better contrast.
Cleaner notifications.

That is not small.
That is your daily life getting easier because you finally found the door they never pointed out.

Just ask AI.
