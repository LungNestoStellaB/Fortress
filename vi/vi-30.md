# Title

# Description

# Clean Script
You wait two weeks for an appointment.

Doctor.
Lawyer.
Bank.
Specialist.
You finally get your slot and then the same thing happens.
Your mind goes blank the moment it starts.

That is expensive.
Even when the appointment is free.

So before you go, you ask AI to help you prepare.
Here is my situation.
What are the key questions?
What details should I bring?
What should I make sure gets answered before I leave?

Now you walk in with a plan.
Not a speech.
A structure.

That means fewer forgotten questions.
Fewer regrets in the car park after.
And a much better chance of getting what you actually came for.

Just ask AI.
