# Clean Script
You buy something new.

Microwave.
Air fryer.
Washing machine.
Comes with a manual thick enough to stop a door.

You only need one answer.
How do I set this thing up without breaking it?

But the manual gives you twelve warnings, six diagrams, and a headache.

So instead of pretending to read page thirty-seven, you take a photo and ask AI the exact question you care about.
How do I start it?
What setting should I use?
What mistake should I avoid?

That is the bridge.
From information overload to one simple next step.

People think AI is about big futuristic things.
Half the magic is smaller than that.
It helps normal people get unstuck.

Just ask AI.
