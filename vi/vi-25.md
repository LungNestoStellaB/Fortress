# Title
The News Story You Did Not Know How To Read

# Description
Fast-moving news often leaves people confused, manipulated, or exhausted. This script shows how AI can separate event, opinion, and implication.

# Clean Script
The news breaks.

Everyone has a take.
Everyone sounds certain.
And within ten minutes you are expected to know what happened, what it means, and which side you are meant to be on.

That is not information.
That is pressure dressed up as urgency.

So now you use AI differently.
You paste the article in.
You ask it to separate fact from opinion.
Summarise what actually happened.
Explain the context.
Tell you what questions are still unanswered.

That gives you breathing room.
You stop being pushed around by noise.
You start seeing structure.

That is the real promise here.
Not that AI thinks for you.
That it helps you think without drowning.

Just ask AI.
