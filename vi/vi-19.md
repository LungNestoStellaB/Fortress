# Title
The Symptom You Should Not Be Googling

# Description
Late-night health anxiety gets worse when search results are vague, extreme, or contradictory. This script positions AI as a calmer first pass before the next proper step.

# Clean Script
You feel something strange.

A rash.
A pain.
A symptom that was not there yesterday.
And suddenly you are doing what everyone does.
Searching the internet and making yourself worse.

Because normal search does not calm you down.
It throws everything at you.
Best case. Worst case. Rare case. Nightmare case.

So instead, you describe the symptom to AI.
Your age.
What changed.
How long it has been happening.
Then you ask a better question.
What are the likely possibilities, and what signs mean I should get help now?

That is a different experience entirely.
Less panic.
More structure.
Better questions for the doctor if you need one.

Not fear.
Clarity.

Just ask AI.
