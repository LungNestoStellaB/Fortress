# Description
Medical language can make smart people feel small. This script gives viewers a clear way to understand a medication before panic, confusion, or silence takes over.

# Clean Script
You come home with a new medication.

Long name.
Tiny print.
Side effects listed like a threat.
And now you are meant to feel calm about swallowing it.

That is not confidence.
That is obedience with better packaging.

So you slow the moment down.
You take a photo of the label and leaflet.
You ask AI what the medicine is for.
What the common side effects are.
What questions you should ask your doctor or pharmacist.

Not to replace them.
To stop walking in blind.

That changes everything.
You stop nodding along.
You start understanding your own care.

For a lot of people, that is the difference between fear and control.

Just ask AI.
