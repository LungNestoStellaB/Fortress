# PW Episode 09 — The King's Garden

## Description
King Bhumibol didn't just talk about sufficiency. He built it. His experimental farms at Chitralada Palace became living proof that the philosophy worked — and a model that spread across Thailand. This episode is about leading by example.

## Clean Script

Inside the walls of Chitralada Palace,
King Bhumibol built a farm.

Not for show.
For proof.

He grew rice in the palace grounds.
Raised fish in the palace ponds.
Tested crops, composting, irrigation —
everything he asked his people to try,
he tried first.

When farmers doubted,
he could say: I know it works.
I grew it myself.

That is the rarest kind of leadership.
Not pointing from a distance.
Standing in the soil beside you.

Whatever you're asking others to do —
do it first.
Whatever change you want to see —
live it first.

The King's garden wasn't just a farm.
It was a promise.
