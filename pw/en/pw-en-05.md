# PW Episode 05 — What the Soil Remembers

## Description
Modern farming forgot something. The soil didn't. For generations, farmers across Asia grew food without chemicals, without debt, without dependency. King Bhumibol spent his life recovering that knowledge — and it's still there, waiting.

## Clean Script

Your grandparents knew something you don't.

They knew which plants grew together.
Which soil needed rest.
Which season to trust and which to wait out.

Then the modern world arrived
with chemicals and credit
and tolm their way was backward.

King Bhumibol disagreed.
He sent teams into the villages.
Not to teach — to listen.
To recover what was almost lost.

That knowledge is still in the soil.
Still in the hands of the elders.
Still available to you.

Before you buy another product,
ask an older farmer.
Ask the land itself.

The answers were always there.
We just stopped listening.
