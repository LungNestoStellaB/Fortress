# PW Episode 10 — One Step Back, Ten Steps Forward

## Description
Sometimes the wisest move is to slow down, pull back, and build the foundation properly before scaling. King Bhumibol called this the "step-by-step" principle — and it's why the communities that followed his philosophy outlasted every crisis.

## Clean Script

Everyone wants to move fast.
Scale quickly. Grow big. Go now.

And then the first storm hits —
and everything built on a weak foundation
comes down.

King Bhumibol taught a different rhythm.
He called it step by step.

Build your foundation first.
Make sure it holds.
Then take the next step.

The farmers who followed this principle
didn't get rich overnight.
But when drought came, they survived.
When prices crashed, they survived.
When the world went sideways — they survived.

Because they built slowly, on solid ground.

One step back to assess.
One step back to strengthen.
Then ten steps forward — and nothing can stop you.

Slow is not weak.
Slow is how you build something that lasts.
