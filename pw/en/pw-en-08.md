# PW Episode 08 — Your Hands Know More Than You Think

## Description
We outsourced everything — cooking, growing, fixing, building. And in doing so, we outsourced our confidence. King Bhumibol believed deeply in practical skill as the foundation of a dignified life. This episode is about reclaiming what your hands already know.

## Clean Script

When did you last fix something with your hands?

Grow something. Build something. Cook something from scratch.

We've been told to specialise.
Leave the rest to experts.
Buy what you need.

And slowly, quietly —
we forgot we were capable.

King Bhumibol spent time in his palace workshops.
He fixed things. He built things. He grew things.
Not because he had to.
Because he believed that a person who can do things
is a person who cannot be made helpless.

Practical skill is not old-fashioned.
It is freedom.

Start small.
Fix one thing.
Grow one thing.
Cook one thing from scratch.

Your hands remember.
Give them the chance to prove it.
