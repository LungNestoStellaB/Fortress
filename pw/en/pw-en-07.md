# PW Episode 07 — The Village That Fed Itself

## Description
One family can grow food. One village can feed itself. King Bhumibol proved this across hundreds of communities in Thailand — not with aid, not with charity, but with cooperation and shared sufficiency. This episode is about the power of community self-reliance.

## Clean Script

One person growing food is survival.
Ten families sharing what they grow — that's a village.

King Bhumibol understood this deeply.
He didn't just teach individual sufficiency.
He built networks.

Villages where one family grew rice,
another raised fish,
another kept bees.
Each one sufficient alone.
Together — abundant.

When drought hit one family,
the village didn't collapse.
It shared.

We've been told that success is individual.
That you rise alone or not at all.

King Bhumibol showed us something different.
The strongest unit isn't the person.
It's the community that knows how to take care of itself.

Who is your village?
