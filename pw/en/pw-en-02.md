# PW Episode 02 — The Enough Trap

## Description
Modern life is built on one lie: that you never have enough. King Bhumibol saw this trap clearly — and taught a different way of measuring wealth. This episode is about knowing when enough is actually enough.

## Clean Script

Every ad you see says the same thing:
you're not there yet.
More. Bigger. Better. Faster.

And so you work harder.
Borrow more.
Chase a finish line that keeps moving.

King Bhumibol watched this happen to his people.
And he said: stop.

Not stop dreaming.
Stop letting someone else define what enough looks like for you.

Sufficiency isn't poverty.
It's clarity.

It's knowing what you actually need,
what truly makes your life full,
and having the courage to stop there.

The trap isn't wanting things.
The trap is never knowing when you've arrived.

Decide what enough looks like.
Then protect it.
