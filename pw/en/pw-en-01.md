# PW Episode 01 — The Seed You Already Have

## Description
Most people wait for the perfect moment, the perfect tools, the perfect conditions. King Bhumibol never did. He started with what was there. This episode reminds us that the seed of a better life is already in your hands.

## Clean Script

The world tells you: wait.
Wait until you have more money.
Wait until you have more space.
Wait until conditions are perfect.

King Bhumibol walked into the hardest villages in Thailand
and he didn't wait.
He knelt down, picked up the soil, and asked:
"What can grow here?"

Not what's missing.
What's already here.

One seed. One patch of ground. One decision to begin.

Sufficiency doesn't start when everything is ready.
It starts the moment you stop waiting.

You already have the seed.
Plant it.
