# PW Episode 06 — Rain Is Not Your Enemy

## Description
We fight nature and wonder why we lose. King Bhumibol spent decades studying water — how to catch it, store it, direct it, and work with it instead of against it. This episode is about the power of alignment over resistance.

## Clean Script

When the rain comes, most people complain.
Too much. Too little. Wrong time.

King Bhumibol never complained about rain.
He studied it.

He built over 4,000 water projects across Thailand.
Not to fight the rain —
to catch it, store it, release it slowly.
To let nature do the work.

The farmers who followed his model
stopped losing crops to floods.
Stopped watching their fields crack in the dry season.

Because they stopped fighting the sky
and started working with it.

Whatever you're struggling against right now —
ask yourself:
am I fighting this, or can I work with it?

Nature doesn't lose.
But it will teach you, if you listen.
