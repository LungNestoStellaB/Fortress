# PW Episode 04 — The Debt That Grows in the Dark

## Description
Debt doesn't announce itself. It grows quietly, in the gaps between what you earn and what you spend. King Bhumibol warned his people about this trap — and showed them how sufficiency is the only real protection.

## Clean Script

You didn't notice when it started.
A small loan here.
A credit card there.
Just to get through the month.

And then one day you realise —
you're working for the debt,
not for yourself.

King Bhumibol saw this destroy families.
Farmers who borrowed for seeds
and lost their land paying back interest.

He said: spend less than you earn.
Save before you need to.
Build a buffer before life tests you.

Not because money is everything.
Because debt in the dark
grows faster than anything you plant.

Sufficiency is your shield.
Build it before you need it.
