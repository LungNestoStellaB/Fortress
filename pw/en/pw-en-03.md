# PW Episode 03 — Grow One Thing

## Description
Change feels impossible when you try to change everything at once. King Bhumibol's philosophy starts smaller than you think — one plant, one skill, one habit. This episode is about the power of beginning with just one thing.

## Clean Script

You want to change your life.
So you make a list.
Exercise. Save money. Learn a skill. Grow your own food.
And then — nothing happens.

Because everything at once is the same as nothing.

King Bhumibol didn't build Thailand's sufficiency movement
with grand plans.
He started with one experimental plot.
One crop. One question: can this grow here?

And it did.

Pick one thing.
One plant on your balcony.
One skill you practice for twenty minutes a day.
One habit you protect like it matters.

Because it does.

Small and real beats big and imaginary every time.

Grow one thing.
Then grow the next.
